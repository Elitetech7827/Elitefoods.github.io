It's our mission to bring to your doorstep whatever meal you desire. Our app is loaded with the best food experiences in the world - Nigerian to Indian to Chinese from all your favorite food vendors. Order your favourite dishes to your doorstep with the Olilifood online delivery app. Whether you're too busy to cook, or you just want to have a different taste of food, whatever you need, Olilifood comes to the rescue!

We know the best food for every cuisine in your area. Olilifood app is the best food delivery service in Delta state - so take the first bite and download now!

First, verify your phone number to get registered on the app. After you're logged in, enter your address (wherever you want the order delivered). Then choose from a variety of restaurants and place an order. The restaurant prepares your order, and once they're ready, our dispatch riders bring them to you. You also get to track your rider till he gets to you. Just sit back and wait for your order!

Olilifood works with the best local restaurants and vendors in Delta state to bring to your doorstep whatever you're after. You do not have to bother about anything once you place your order, and you can go about your other activities.

Olilifood is the most recent online food delivery in Delta State, but is rising to take over the online food delivery market. Olilifood is here to challenge the efficiency of the food delivery industry all over the world, while also helping the restaurants get more orders.
